About Me

I started out in the science field by studying Ecology at Colorado State University (CSU). I took up many extracurriculars such as wildlife clubs, the student body government, and I Participated in two research conferences where I conducted and presented my own studies, The Front Range Ecology Symposium, and the Celebrate Undergraduate Research and Creativity (CURC) Showcase. During my final year in college I had my first exposure to the world of data modeling and science. I worked as a research assistant to a Graduate student using python to create a interactive, ecological, data model. This is what sparked my interest in Data Science.

After graduating I took time to take some coursework in computer science to learn some basics and, programming. I currently am working on a certification in Full stack developement to strengthen my coding and project management while also working on side project in data science for practice. The next goal is General Assembly Data Science bootcamp and working on commitioned projects for more practice. Eventually, I hope to return to get a Masters in Datascience.
